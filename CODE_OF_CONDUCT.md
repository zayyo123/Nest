# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Participants are expected to communicate with respect, assume good intent, and
keep discussions focused on improving the project.

Examples of welcome behavior:

- Being kind and direct in feedback
- Explaining tradeoffs clearly
- Respecting different experience levels
- Keeping issues and pull requests constructive

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Sharing private information without permission
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussion
- Discriminatory language or behavior

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation
when behavior harms the project or its community.
